FAQ

How do I reset my workspace?

Open a challenge, then use the Reset button to clear blocks and tests for that challenge.

Can I export my solutions?

Yes. Use Export in the app to download JSON for your current challenge. Import restores it later.

Can I view the generated code?

Enable “Show code” in the app to see the JavaScript produced by your blocks.

Does it work offline?

After the first load, yes, except the Blockly CDN. Vendor the bundle for full offline use.
